About Us

The goal of the Pet-Place is to have a place for pet owners to go and get reliable information on their pets. We find that there is a large amount of misinformation regarding topics on pets. There are many uncited statements and vague unhelpful tips about pet care on the internet, that it gets hard to tell what to trust. Here at the Pet-Place, we aim to bring useful and reliable information on topics such as feeding, bonding, and even breed specific care straight to you!

We are a small team of three senior Texas A&M Kingsville students (Sean Crosby, Robyn Barrera, and Kekoa Madiera) dedicated to making a website that helps you take care of your beloved companions. We carefully pick through information, and we only give you the most accurate information. Your pet's happiness and wellbeing are at the heart of all that we do!
